Mechanical Engineering ( ME )

Programme Structure : ( Mechanical )
It is a semester based 4-year undergraduate program which comprises of eight semesters. The course bridges the gap between institute and industry by emphasizing on latest computer applications. NIT runs this course under affiliation with RTMNU, Nagpur. The course provides a foundation in mathematics, computer science and management skills applied to computer science. In this course the students will be exposed to various areas of machine applications including the latest developments keeping abreast with the industry The Institute has well-equipped lab for the department with the latest software and computers.


Machanical Engg. is a 4-year full time degree programme which includes 8 semesters with training (industrial) after 5th semester. The UG degree is awarded by RTMNU, Nagpur.
Eligibility Criteria : Candidate of any stream (Science, Commerce or Arts) securing 50% or more (45 % marks only for SC/ST/OBC candidate) at 10+2 class. Student must be required intermediate Knowledge of Computer operator.

  1. Reservation of seats is strictly according to the Maharshtra State Government guidelines.
  2. Management Quota/NRI 25% seats have been reserved as the management quota.
  3. Admission to these seats will be made at the institute level based on merit.

Course Information

B.Tech(ME) is Full-time Technical UG course. The duration is 4 years ( 8 semester ).

B.Tech subjects include Manufacturing process, Thermodynamics, Kinematics, Control systems, Web technology and computer languages such as HTML & Css, C, C++, C#, Java.

FAQ

What type of jobs will be offered after the B.Tech ?

After the B.Tech student can select the field of Machine development, website designing & developing, digital marketing, software testing, database administrator, BPO, Banking jobs, Government jobs, etc.

What is the admission procedure to take admission in B.Tech ?

Candidate eligible for admission will fill the online application form available on the college website and pay a registration fee of Rs. 1,000/- to take provisional admission. We can not be force to pay complete fees at the time of admission.

Candidate can offers to pay annual fees in two installment.
