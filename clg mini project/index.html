About Our NIT

NAGPUR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY ( NIT )

The Nagpur Institute of Technology is one of the oldest Management Institute in Nagpur. Established in the year 2008 ,by The Sai sikshan sanstha.

The Institute conducts regular Under Graduate programs like B.tech & polytechniq under affiliation of RTMNU.
